MISSION

Our mission is to help students identify and actualize their unique
career and educational goals.

Through individualized advisement, Exploration of resources, and
opportunities for experiential education, we work to introduce career
pathways and future employment opportunities to students and alumni.

Ultimately preparing them for postgraduate and workforce success.


VISION

To support the overall student-centered mission of the College and
empower students to forge a career path that best fits their interests,
personality, values, and overall life goals.

Established in 2010 as part of the Alternative Learning System (ALS), the Unified Internship Program (UIP) initially facilitated placements for graduating students in various companies. However, the COVID-19 pandemic disrupted traditional internships, prompting UIP to pivot to remote learning. Now operating as a non-profit organization entirely online, UIP offers work-from-home internship options to meet the evolving needs of students and partner institutions. Led by dedicated professionals, we prioritize addressing program requirements through online connections and distance learning.
